# CarbonFlow strategic wedges (12–18 months)

This document fixes **2–3 wedges** so roadmap work compounds instead of spreading thinly. It sequences delivery in **four horizons** (roughly quarters).

## Wedges (prioritized)

### 1. Regional disclosure excellence (India + EU)

**Why:** BRSR, CBAM, and CSRD/ESRS drive budget and urgency in two large markets you already touch (INR pricing, compliance modes).

**Sequence:**

- **Months 0–4:** Disclosure pack exports (CDP-style + ESRS skeleton), methodology visibility, inventory snapshots (done in product).
- **Months 4–8:** Section-level completeness scoring vs framework; regulatory calendar (deadlines + gaps).
- **Months 8–12:** Deeper ESRS datapoint coverage (E1 climate) and auditor-friendly evidence bundles.

### 2. Scope 3 + supplier motion

**Why:** Differentiation vs pure finance-led tools; defensible footprint for mid-market manufacturers and brands.

**Sequence:**

- **Months 0–4:** Supplier engagement, network, intelligence (existing)—tighten data quality scores and benchmarks.
- **Months 4–8:** Category-specific templates (purchased goods, transport lanes), tier-2 ask workflows.
- **Months 8–12:** PCF/BOM-lite for key SKUs (optional vertical).

### 3. API-first automation & trust

**Why:** Becomes the system of record for ops and partners; wins technical buyers.

**Sequence:**

- **Months 0–4:** Public/enterprise APIs, webhooks, idempotent bulk ingestion contracts; **factor versioning + lineage** exposed in API.
- **Months 4–8:** Connector roadmap execution (shortlist in `INTEGRATIONS_SHORTLIST.md`).
- **Months 8–12:** Assurance workflow (auditor role, evidence requests)—after lineage is trusted.

## What we deprioritize early

- **Connector sprawl** before factor versioning and audit lineage are credible (avoids “black box integrations”).
- **Generic AI** before numbers are grounded and cited in-product.

## Success metrics (examples)

- Time to “period closed” inventory (snapshot + disclosure export).
- Supplier response rate and Scope 3 completeness %.
- API adoption (keys active, sync success rate).
